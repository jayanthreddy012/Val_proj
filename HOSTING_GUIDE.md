## ‚≠ê OPTION 1: GitHub Pages (Recommended - 100% Free Forever)

**Step-by-step:**

1. **Create a GitHub account** (if you don't have one)
   - Go to https://github.com/signup

2. **Create a new repository**
   - Click the "+" icon ‚Üí "New repository"
   - Name it: `valentine-surprise` (or any name)
   - Make it **Public**
   - Check "Add a README file"
   - Click "Create repository"

3. **Upload your files**
   - Click "Add file" ‚Üí "Upload files"
   - Drag and drop BOTH files:
     - `valentines_dinner.html`
     - `celebration_dance.gif`
   - Click "Commit changes"

4. **Rename the HTML file**
   - Click on `valentines_dinner.html`
   - Click the pencil icon (Edit)
   - Change the name to `index.html`
   - Click "Commit changes"

5. **Enable GitHub Pages**
   - Go to "Settings" tab
   - Click "Pages" in left sidebar
   - Under "Source", select "main" branch
   - Click "Save"

6. **Get your link!**
   - Wait 1-2 minutes
   - Your site will be at: `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/valentine-surprise/`

## üåü OPTION 2: Netlify Drop (Easiest - No Account Needed!)

**Super simple:**

1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop

2. Drag and drop BOTH files directly onto the page:
   - `valentines_dinner.html`
   - `celebration_dance.gif`

3. **IMPORTANT:** After upload, rename `valentines_dinner.html` to `index.html` in the Netlify interface

4. Get your instant link! (looks like: `https://random-name.netlify.app`)

5. Optional: Create a free account to keep the site permanently and customize the URL
